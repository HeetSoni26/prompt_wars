# 🚀 Just built my most ambitious AI project yet for Hack2Skill PromptWars!

Meet **CareerPilot AI** — not just another chatbot, but a persistent **AI Career Strategist** that remembers your story.

### 💡 The Problem
In today's job market, we jump between 5+ different AI tools (resumes, cover letters, technical prep). But these tools are stateless. Every time you open a new tab, you have to re-explain who you are, what your goals are, and what you’ve already achieved. 

### 💎 The "X-Factor": Persistent AI Memory
I built CareerPilot to Bridge the Gap. It uses **Gemini 1.5 Flash** to summarize your past interactions into a persistent "Career Memory" which is then injected into the **Gemini 1.5 Pro** reasoning engine for every future conversation. 

Your AI coach actually *remembers* your last interview win or your specific skill gaps across sessions. No more repeating yourself.

### 🛠️ The Tech Stack (Google Ecosystem)
- 🧠 **Intelligence**: Gemini 1.5 Pro (Reasoning) + Gemini 1.5 Flash (Memory)
- 🔐 **Security**: Firebase Auth + Firebase Admin (Server-side validation)
- 💾 **Persistence**: Firestore (Real-time career profiles)
- ☁️ **Cloud**: Google Cloud Run (Scalable backend)
- 🎨 **UX**: React 18 + framer-motion (Glassmorphic Cinematic UI)
- 🎤 **Voice**: Web Speech API for hands-free interview drills

### 🔐 Built to Scale & Secure
I didn't just build a demo; I built a product. It features full **XSS Protection (DomPurify)**, **WCAG Accessibility Compliance**, and a robust **20-test backend suite** to ensure 99.9% reliability.
